Clean, distraction-free recipe view with cook mode features.

  • Screen stays awake in cook mode
  • Share button exports to other apps
  • Delete/edit in overflow menu

Meal Planner

Weekly calendar view for planning meals with saved recipes.

  • Day selector shows meal status at a glance

  • Tap + opens recipe picker modal
  • Generate shopping list from planned meals
  • Long-press to remove assigned recipe

Auto-generated, consolidated list from meal plan recipes.

  • Grouped by category (produce, protein, pantry)

  • Source attribution shows which recipe needs it
  • Tap to check off items while shopping
  • Share exports as text or to reminders app
  • Manual add for extra items
  • Persists offline in SQLite
